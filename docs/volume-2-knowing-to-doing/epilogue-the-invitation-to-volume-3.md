# Epilogue: The Invitation to Volume 3

I want to be clear about how I see the entry requirement for Volume 3.

Volume 3 is not a harder read or a more advanced intellectual framework. It is a different level of ambition entirely. Volume 1 described the laws. Volume 2 described the conditions under which a person can actually operate under those laws. Volume 3 asks what it looks like to live a life in which miracles are the normal — not the exceptional — outcome. A life in which I am beginning to quantify the spiritual forces at work, to see the equations underneath the experiences, to model the feedback systems that govern how the Spirit moves in and through my specific life.

The entry requirement for Volume 3 is not intellectual. I do not need to have understood everything in Volumes 1 and 2 theoretically. I believe I need to have experience in at least these three things:

- The release of at least one significant emotional knot through the ministry of the Holy Spirit. Not a moment of emotional catharsis — the actual release of a knot: the valley leveling, the bandwidth returning, the old pull no longer pulling in the same way. If you have not experienced this, Part II of this volume has the tools. Start there.
- The experience of hearing a specific word and obeying it and seeing a result in the natural. Not a vague sense of being led, but a specific word, a specific act of obedience, and a specific observable consequence. The five-for-five batting average that Larry described from those Saturday mornings. Even once is enough to change the framework.
- The experience of genuine community correction — bringing something I thought I had heard to a group of trusted brothers and being told they did not witness it as true — and finding that the correction was right. This experience is humbling and essential. It is what teaches me to hold my hearing loosely and submit it for testing.
If you have experienced all three, you are ready for Volume 3. Not because you are spiritually advanced — but because you have moved from knowing the laws intellectually to knowing them in the way Principle 3 demands: in your spirit, in your mind and emotions, and in your body and actions. That is the foundation Volume 3 needs to build on.

If you have not experienced all three yet, that is not a failure. It is simply a description of where you are on the journey, and the journey is the point. Stay in the practices of Part III. Keep the training plan. Stay in community. Clear the knots as they surface. The experiences will come — not as rewards for performance, but as the natural fruit of a channel that is increasingly open and a heart whose soil is increasingly good.

Volume 3 is further up and further in. I will meet you there.

*Blessings — Daddy and Granddaddy*
