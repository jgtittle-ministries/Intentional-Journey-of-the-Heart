# Third Exploration: Spiritual Force — The Analytical Core

## Starting Where the Data Is
The right place to start the quantitative program is not with the most ambitious equations but with the best-documented data points. In the IJH project, the best-documented causal chain is the Rom 10:17 sequence: Word → Hearing → Faith. This is a causal statement with an identifiable input (the Word of Christ), an identifiable intermediate state (genuine hearing), and an identifiable output (faith, operationalized as trust). It is, in systems dynamics language, a stock-and-flow relationship: faith is a stock that accumulates over time through the inflow of genuine hearing.

The first question for Vol. 3 is: What force drives this flow? What pushes faith into the stock? And what are the forces that drain it?

## The Mustard Seed: Threshold or Proportionality? Resolution Required.

![](images/image-004.jpeg)

![](images/image-002.jpeg)

***Matt. 17:20 (ESV)***

*“He said to them, “Because of your little faith. For truly, I say to you, if you have faith like a grain of mustard seed, you will say to this mountain, ‘Move from here to there,’ and it will move, and nothing will be impossible for you.”*

I flagged this as unresolved in Vol 1. It must be resolved here before the equations can be built, because the two candidate models produce fundamentally different equation forms.

Model A (Threshold): A mustard seed of genuine faith is sufficient for any miracle. The constraint on miracles is quality, not quantity — whether the faith is genuine trust or merely intellectual assent. If this model is right, the equation for spiritual force as a function of faith is a step function: effectively zero below the threshold of genuine trust, full effective force above it.

Model B (Proportionality): The mustard seed statement is a lower bound, not a ceiling. More genuine faith produces more spiritual force. If this model is right, the equation is more like F_s = k × f(trust), where trust is a continuous variable and the spiritual force scales continuously with it.

My current position: Model A is more consistent with the scriptural data. Jesus rebukes his disciples for having no faith or little faith, which reads more naturally as a quality failure (fear-mixed trust, intellectual assent without genuine submission) than a quantity failure. The centurion data point (Matt 8) strongly supports this: the operative element in what Jesus calls “great faith” is the purity and precision of his trust — he understands how authority works because he operates in the same structure himself.

However, a synthesis is possible and may be closer to the truth: there is a threshold of genuine trust below which spiritual force cannot operate at all (Model A for the floor), and above that threshold, the precision and alignment of faith with God’s specific will in the specific moment (not the quantity of faith abstractly) determines the magnitude of the outcome. This is the resonance model, and it connects directly to the Prayer as Resonance law from Vol 1.

**TFT CONNECTION: ***In TFT terms, faith (trust) is the field excitation that moves the system through configuration space. The mustard seed threshold corresponds to the minimum excitation energy needed to escape a **local minimum** in the potential landscape. Below this threshold, the system stays trapped in its current configuration. Above it, movement toward the Glory attractor becomes possible. The precision of faith — aligning with what God is **actually doing** (the Vol 1 Obedience Channel law) — is what determines the direction of movement.*

## The Five Scriptural Data Points for Spiritual Force

![](images/image-010.png)

### Data Point 1: The Rom 10:17 Chain
Spiritual force flows from the Word of Christ into genuine hearing into faith. This is the input-output structure of the spiritual force generator. The force is not created by me; it is received. My role is to keep the channel clear (Vol 2) and genuinely open (not merely intellectual) to what the Word is saying.

### Data Point 2: The Centurion — Force Multiplier through Authority Alignment
***Matt. 8:8-10 (ESV)***

*“But the centurion replied, “Lord, I am not worthy to have you come under my roof, but only say the word, and my servant will be healed. For I too am a man under authority, with soldiers under me. And I say to one, ‘Go,’ and he goes, and to another, ‘Come,’ and he comes, and to my servant, **‘Do this,’ and he does it.” When Jesus heard this, he marveled and said to those who followed him, “Truly, I tell you, with no one in Israel have I found such faith.”*

This is the Vol 1 Spiritual Authority law expressed as a force multiplier. The centurion understands that authority structures amplify force — that a word spoken within a proper authority chain produces effects that the same word spoken outside that chain cannot produce. Jesus’ astonishment is the data point: no one else has understood this. The equation implication: F_(effective) = F_(base+ × g(authority alignment), where g is a multiplier function that equals 1 at baseline and increases as the speaker operates more fully within delegated spiritual authority.

Two other authority data points are negative examples: when the father sins, the family suffers, and when the king sins, the nation suffers.

### Data Point 3: Elijah’s Seven Prayers — Resonance Accumulation
***1 Kgs. 18:43-44 (ESV)***

*“And he said to his servant, “Go up now, look toward the sea.” And he went up and looked and said, “There is nothing.” And he said, “Go again,” seven times. And at the seventh time he said, “Behold, a little cloud like a man’s hand is rising from the sea.””*

Seven prayers, each in the same direction, each building on the last, until the cloud appears. This is the resonance data point. In physical resonance, a small force applied repeatedly at the natural frequency of a system produces increasingly large oscillations — not because the force gets larger, but because the timing aligns with the system’s own dynamics and the energy accumulates rather than dissipating. Elijah is praying in alignment with what he has already heard God declare (v.1: “I will send rain”) — he is not trying to change God’s mind; he is aligning his prayer with God’s stated intention and persisting until the accumulation reaches the threshold of natural manifestation.

The equation implication: repeated prayer aligned with God’s stated will produces accumulating spiritual force, not because each prayer is additive in a simple sense, but because alignment + persistence = resonance, and resonance produces outsized results relative to the individual force applied.

### Data Point 4: James 5:16 — The “As It Is Working” Language
***James 5:16 (ESV)***

*“Therefore, confess your sins to one another and pray for one another, that you may be healed. The prayer of a righteous person has great power as it is working.”*

The phrase “as it is working” translates the Greek energoumene — being energized, being in active operation. This does not describe a static quantity of prayer force but a dynamic process: prayer that is in active alignment with the Spirit’s work has great power precisely because it is in that active state. The force is not stored; it flows when the channel is open and aligned. This supports the resonance model and points toward the concept of spiritual power as distinct from spiritual force — power being the rate at which force produces work.

### Data Point 5: The Woman with the Issue of Blood — Directional Force Flow
***Mark 5:30 (ESV)***

*“And Jesus, perceiving in himself that power had gone out from him, immediately turned about in the crowd and said, “Who touched my garments?”*

Jesus perceived power flowing out of Him in a specific direction toward a specific person whose faith had reached the threshold for receiving it. This is one of the most remarkable data points in the gospels from a Vol 3 perspective. It implies: (1) spiritual power is directional, not omnidirectional; (2) it is perceptible, at least to Jesus; (3) it flows in response to faith, not merely to need — many others were touching and pressing on Jesus but only one drew the power. The force is not broadcast; it flows along the channel of faith.

**TFT CONNECTION:** In* TFT terms, this is the gauge field mediating the interaction: Light (the visibility condition) combined with Love (the coupling constant) creates a directed connection between two nodes in the relational field. The woman’s faith creates the connection; the connection enables the force transfer. Power — as TFT classifies it, the potential energy or driving term in the Lagrangian — flows from the source (Jesus as the point of maximum field intensity in the configuration space) toward the receiving node whose position in configuration space has opened sufficiently to accept the transfer.*
